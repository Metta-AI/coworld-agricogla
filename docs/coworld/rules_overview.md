# Agricogla — rules overview

14 rounds of worker placement. Each round every player places their family
members one at a time on open action spaces (one worker per space, turn
order clockwise from the starting player). Harvests happen after rounds
**4, 7, 9, 11, 13 and 14**: fields yield one crop each, every adult family
member must be fed **2 food** (newborns 1), and each animal pair breeds one
offspring. Each missing food is a begging card worth **−3 points**.

## The action board

Fixed spaces offer resources (wood, clay, reed, stone, food, grain,
vegetables), building (rooms/stables, fields, fences, renovation),
occupations, improvements and the starting-player marker. One new
round-card space is revealed each round — sheep, then fences/improvements,
then boar, cattle, family growth, renovation and late-game actions —
in a stage-ordered shuffle. Accumulating spaces (wood, clay, reed, fishing,
animals…) gain goods every round until someone takes the whole pile.

Family growth requires a room per family member; renovation upgrades the
house wood → clay → stone. Animals need capacity: pastures (bigger and
subdivided hold more), stables (double a pasture; one loose animal can live
in an unfenced stable) and one pet in the house. Animals that cannot be
housed are cooked (with a fireplace/hearth) or released.

## Cards

Each player is dealt 7 occupations and 7 minor improvements (this port's
own 51 + 48 card deck on the canonical archetypes). Occupations cost food
to play (first one free on the main lessons space); minors cost resources
and check prerequisites. Ten major improvements (fireplaces, hearths,
ovens, well, joinery/pottery/basketmaker's workshop) are open for purchase.
Cooking improvements convert animals, grain and vegetables to food; ovens
bake bread.

## Scoring (after round 14)

| Category | −1 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fields | 0–1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5+ |
| Pastures | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4+ |
| Grain (supply + on fields) | 0 | 1–3 | 4–5 | 6–7 | 8+ |
| Vegetables | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4+ |
| Sheep | 0 | 1–3 | 4–5 | 6–7 | 8+ |
| Wild boar | 0 | 1–2 | 3–4 | 5–6 | 7+ |
| Cattle | 0 | 1 | 2–3 | 4–5 | 6+ |

Plus −1 per unused farmyard space, +1 per fenced stable, +1 per clay room,
+2 per stone room, +3 per family member, −3 per begging card, and printed
victory/bonus points on played cards. Highest total wins; the Coworld
results report a tie as `winner: -1`.
